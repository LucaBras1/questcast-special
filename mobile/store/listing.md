## App Title

## Short Description (80 characters max)

## Full Description (4000 characters max)

**Questcast** is an AI-powered voice RPG that puts a personal Dungeon Master in your pocket. No scripts. No pre-written stories. Just you, your voice, and an AI that reacts to everything you say.

**HOW IT WORKS**
Tap the crystal orb and speak your actions. Your AI Dungeon Master listens, then narrates the consequences in rich, dramatic voice. Every session is unique — your choices shape the story in real time.

**FEATURES**

Speak to Play
Tap the crystal orb, say what you want to do, and hear the story unfold. No typing required. Voice-first interaction makes every session feel like a real tabletop RPG.

AI Dungeon Master
Powered by advanced AI that remembers your choices, tracks your character, and creates branching narratives. The story adapts to YOU — every time.

Character Creation
Choose from four classes: Warrior, Mage, Rogue, or Ranger. Each with unique starting stats, abilities, and gear. Name your hero and step into the adventure.

Dice Rolling
Real RPG mechanics with animated dice rolls. Critical hits, fumbles, and everything between. The dice decide your fate in combat and challenges.

Combat System
Face dangerous enemies in dramatic encounters. Choose to attack, defend, use abilities, or flee. Every battle is narrated with cinematic flair.

Save & Continue
Your adventures save automatically. Pick up exactly where you left off, with a recap of what happened last time.

AI-Generated Scene Art
At key story moments, the AI generates unique artwork depicting your scene. See your adventure come to life.

Session Timer
Built-in session timer keeps track of your play time. Adventure at your own pace.

**PRIVACY & DATA**
Your voice recordings are processed to understand your commands and are not stored permanently. We take your privacy seriously. See our full privacy policy for details.

**AI DISCLOSURE**
Questcast uses artificial intelligence to generate game narratives, character interactions, and scene descriptions. AI-generated content may occasionally be unexpected or imperfect. Content is filtered for safety and appropriateness.

- Creative minds who love collaborative storytelling

## AI Disclosure Statement (Google Play Requirement)
This application uses artificial intelligence (OpenAI GPT-4o-mini) to generate game narratives, character dialogues, and story responses in real time. Voice input is processed using AI speech recognition (OpenAI Whisper). AI text-to-speech is used for narration voice output. Scene images are generated using AI image generation (DALL-E 3). All AI-generated content is filtered through safety moderation systems. AI-generated content may occasionally produce unexpected results. No AI-generated content is presented as human-created.
