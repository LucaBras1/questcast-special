<visual_identity>
Questcast Visual Brand -- All generated images must share this consistent look:
Color palette: Warm golds, amber, and ochre for safety and hope. Deep purples, indigo, and midnight blue for mystery and danger. Crimson and burnt orange for conflict and urgency. All scenes carry a torch-lit warmth even in darkness.
Style: Digital painting, semi-realistic with painterly brushwork. Not photorealistic, not cartoonish. Think concept art for a AAA fantasy game. Rich textures, visible brushstrokes at edges, sharp focus on the focal point with softer atmospheric backgrounds.
Mood: Epic fantasy with gravitas. Every image should feel like a frame from a cinematic adventure. Dramatic lighting with strong directional light sources such as torches, moonlight, magical glow, or dawn breaking through clouds. Deep shadows with warm edge light.
Scale: Environments feel vast and ancient. Structures are weathered. Nature is wild and untamed. The player character is never depicted directly to preserve player imagination.
</visual_identity>
