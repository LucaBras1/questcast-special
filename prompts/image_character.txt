<visual_identity>
Questcast Visual Brand -- Character portraits share the same visual language as scene images:
Color palette: Warm golds and amber for skin highlights and armor accents. Deep purples and indigo for shadows and background. Crimson or burnt orange for accent details like cloaks, gems, or magical effects.
Style: Digital painting, semi-realistic with painterly brushwork. Not photorealistic, not cartoonish. Concept art quality. Rich textures on armor, fabric, and skin. Visible brushstrokes at edges, sharp focus on the face and shoulders.
Mood: Heroic and dramatic. The character should feel like someone with a story. Dramatic single-source lighting, typically warm torchlight from one side creating deep shadows on the other. The portrait should feel like a painting hanging in a fantasy guild hall.
</visual_identity>
