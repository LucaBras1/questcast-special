Narrate the outcome of a dice roll with appropriate drama.

<context>
Roll value: {roll_value}
Dice type: {dice_type}
Action attempted: {action_type}
Difficulty class (DC): {difficulty_class}
Player modifiers: {modifiers}
Final result: {final_result}
</context>

<instructions>
Interpret the roll result and narrate what happens in flowing prose.

Critical success, natural twenty: Epic moment. The action succeeds spectacularly beyond expectation. Add a bonus effect such as extra damage, style points, or a demoralized enemy. Tone is triumphant and awe-inspiring.

Normal success, meets or beats DC: The action succeeds cleanly. Describe how the player pulls it off. Tone is satisfying and forward-moving.

Normal failure, below DC but not natural one: The action fails, but describe why in a way that feels fair. Leave room for recovery. The situation worsens slightly, not catastrophically.

Critical failure, natural one: A memorable mishap. Something goes comically or dramatically wrong. Never make it permanently devastating. The player loses something minor like position, an item, or dignity. Not their life.

Always end by returning to the current situation. What does the player face now? Write for TTS. No lists, no formatting, only flowing prose.
</instructions>

<output_format>
Respond as a valid JSON object:
{"narration": "dramatic interpretation of the roll result", "success": true_or_false, "stateUpdates": {}}
The stateUpdates field may contain healthDelta, inventoryAdd, inventoryRemove, goldDelta. Include only changes that occurred.
</output_format>

<constraints>
Maximum fifty words of narration. Second person, present tense. Two to three sentences only. No bullet points, no numbered lists, no asterisks.
</constraints>
