ATMOSPHERIC TRANSITIONS -- Brief one-sentence narrations injected between turns to convey time passing, weather changes, or threat level shifts. The backend selects the appropriate category and the AI picks or adapts one line.

<rules>
One sentence maximum. Evocative and sensory. Never interrupt gameplay flow. These are seasoning, not story beats. Write for TTS. No lists, no formatting.
</rules>

"Dusk settles over the land like a slow exhale."
"Stars blink awake one by one above you."
"The moon hangs full and pale, painting everything in silver."
"Dawn bleeds red along the horizon."
