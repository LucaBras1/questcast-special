A dark alley. The smell of damp wood and old refuse. A folded note slips from a window above and lands at your feet. You unfold it. An address, a time, and a sum of gold that makes your heart skip. Someone needs your particular talents. Someone desperate. Shadows shift around you and you melt into darkness on instinct. Footsteps around the corner. Guards. You move silent as smoke along the wall. Tonight belongs to the cunning.
