| Long videos | 1x/týden | YouTube | Deep engagement |

- Community engagement

**Reddit**

## BOFU (Bottom of Funnel) - Conversion

### Onboarding Optimization
1. **First 30 seconds:** WOW moment (AI speaks)
2. **First 5 minutes:** Complete mini-adventure
3. **First session:** Full 45-min free game
4. **Day 1:** Push notification with cliffhanger reminder

### Conversion Tactics

**Cliffhanger Upsell**
- AI ends story at peak tension
- Immediate offer: +15 min for $0.99
- Show subscription value

**Time-Limited Offers**
- 50% off first month (after 3 free sessions)
- Annual plan discount (2 months free)
- Holiday specials

**Social Proof**
- In-app review prompts (after positive session)
- User testimonials
- "X players currently adventuring"

# 5. Retention Marketing

## Email/Push Notification Strategy

### Automated Sequences

**Onboarding (Days 1-7)**
| Day | Message | Goal |
|-----|---------|------|
| 0 | Welcome + quick start guide | Activation |
| 1 | "Your adventure awaits..." | Return |
| 3 | Tips for better gameplay | Education |
| 5 | "Try multiplayer!" | Feature discovery |
| 7 | Special offer (if not converted) | Conversion |

**Re-engagement (Lapsed users)**
| Trigger | Message |
|---------|---------|
| 3 days inactive | "Your quest misses you" |
| 7 days inactive | "New story available!" |
| 14 days inactive | "Special comeback offer: 50% off" |
| 30 days inactive | "We've improved! Come see" |

### Push Notification Best Practices
- Max 1 push/day
- Personalized (use game context)
- Opt-in only (respect preferences)
- A/B test timing and copy

## Loyalty & Referral Program

### Referral Program
- **Referrer gets:** 1 free premium session
- **Referee gets:** Extended first game (60 min)
- **Tracking:** Unique referral codes
- **Target:** 20% of new users from referrals

### Loyalty Rewards
- Streak bonuses (7 days = extra images)
- Monthly challenges
- Exclusive seasonal content for subscribers
- Early access to new features
